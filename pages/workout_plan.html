Your Workout Plan

Weekly Schedule

  • Day 1 – Upper Body Push-ups, Bench Press, Shoulder Press, Triceps Dips
  • Day 2 – Lower Body Squats, Lunges, Leg Press, Calf Raises
  • Day 3 – Rest / Light Cardio Walking or cycling (30 minutes)
  • Day 4 – Full Body Deadlifts, Pull-ups, Plank, Dumbbell Rows
  • Day 5 – Core Crunches, Leg Raises, Russian Twists, Plank

General Guidelines

  • Warm up for 5–10 minutes before training
  • Rest 60–90 seconds between sets
  • Train 3–5 times per week
  • Stay hydrated and sleep at least 7 hours
